# Violence & Physical Harm


## Content to Flag

- **Threatening behavior or language**: Characters threatening to harm others
- **Physical altercations**: Fighting, hitting, or other violence (even if not graphic)
- **Frightening scenarios**: Content that may induce fear or anxiety
- **Weapons**: References to weapons (knives, axes, etc.) even in historical context
- **Injury or death**: References to characters being hurt or dying

**Severity levels:**
- CRITICAL: Graphic or detailed violence, threats against children
- HIGH: Physical altercations, explicit threats, death of characters
- MEDIUM: Mild conflict, historical violence with context, fantasy violence
- LOW: Slapstick humor, very mild conflict resolution
