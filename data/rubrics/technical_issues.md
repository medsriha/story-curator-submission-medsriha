# Technical & Pedagogical Issues

## Content to Flag

- **Formatting artifacts**: Stray characters like '@', markup errors
- **Unclear target audience**: Mismatch between complexity and stated grade
- **Factual errors**: Incorrect information presented as fact
- **Confusing structure**: Text that is hard to follow
- **Missing context**: References requiring outside knowledge

## Review Approach

Flag technical issues that interfere with comprehension or pedagogical effectiveness. Consider whether the content delivers on its educational promise.
