# Cultural Sensitivity & Representation

## Content to Flag

- **Stereotypes**: Character portrayals based on race, gender, nationality, etc.
- **Outdated terminology**: Historical terms that are now considered offensive
- **Misrepresentation**: Inaccurate or disrespectful portrayal of cultures
- **Bias in roles**: Limited or stereotypical gender/cultural roles
- **Othering language**: Language that creates "us vs. them" dynamics
