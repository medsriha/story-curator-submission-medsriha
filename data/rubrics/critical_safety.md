# Critical Safety Restrictions (Automatic Failure)

Content containing any of the following must be flagged for immediate removal or revision.

## Zero Tolerance Items

- Profanity or explicit language
- Sexual content or innuendo
- Hate speech or slurs targeting any group
- Graphic violence or gore
- Weapons with specific reference to modern firearms

## Usage

These items should result in automatic CRITICAL severity flags and require immediate action before publication.
